Who am I?

My name is Jason Berney, I am 25 years old, currently located in Seattle, WA.

Jason graduated from the University of Redlands in April, 2015 with a B.S. in Environmental Science and a Minor in Spatial Studies. While at Redlands he incorporated GIS into making more informed decisions on work that involved land use projects, environmental injustice, natural resources, and physical geography. He has volunteered and interned at non-profit organizations, government agencies, and academic institutions where he has used GIS to support sustainable solutions. In 2016 Jason is pursued a Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies at the University of Washington. Jason is currently employed by Integral GIS, a consulting firm in Seattle using geospatial technologies to visulaize business resources and develop solutions to unique business challenges.
