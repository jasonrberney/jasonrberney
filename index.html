Who am I?

My name is Jason Berney. I am 25 years old, currently located in Seattle, WA.

I graduated from the University of Redlands in April, 2015 with a B.S. in Environmental Science and a Minor in Spatial Studies. While at Redlands I incorporated GIS into making more informed decisions on work that involved land use projects, environmental injustice, natural resources, and physical geography. I have volunteered and interned at non-profit organizations, government agencies, and academic institutions where I've used GIS to support sustainable solutions. In 2016 I pursued a Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies at the University of Washington. I am currently employed by Integral GIS, a consulting firm in Seattle using geospatial technologies to visualize business resources and develop solutions to unique business challenges.
