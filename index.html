Biography

My name is Jason Berney, I am a 22 year old male from Portland, OR.

Jason Berney graduated from the University of Redlands in April, 2015 with a B.S. in Environmental Science and a Minor in Spatial Studies. While at Redlands he incorporated GIS into making more informed decisions on work that involved lan use projects, environmental injustice, natural resources, and physical geography. He has volunteered and interned at non-profit organizations, government agencies, and academic institutions where he has used GIS to support sustainable solutions. In the 2015-2016 academic year Jason is pursuing a Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies degree at the University of Washington. Jason is employed by Integral GIS, a consulting firm in Seattle solving business problems through the use of geospatial technologies.
