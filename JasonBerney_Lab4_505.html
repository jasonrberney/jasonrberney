Des Moines Landslide

This is a 3D representation of a landslide that took place in Des Moines Washington. The data shows the slope of the landslide as well as a mosaic of the landscape taken from a DJI Phantom UAV. A downfall to the web scene viewer is that it does do not support 2D data. Attempts at converting contour polylines of Des Moines to a 3D Feature Class were unsuccessful. The web scene viewer provides a simple, fast and attractive way to view 3D data. Click on the eye in the TOC to the right to turn layers on and off.
