Interests & Experiences...

The following is a blog dedicated to my life experiences and the things that are important to me. Posts are likely to include travel, family and friends, music and more! Cheers!

December 2017. Come From Away.

I was recently in New York City. My uncle lives in Manhattan and has what sounds like the coolest job on planet earth – a Broadway Company Manager. He always has recommendations for the best plays to see when I’m back east. This time around I saw a musical called Come From Away. It told the true story of what happened to 38 aircraft and 7,000 passengers who were stranded in the small town of Gander Newfoundland on 9/11. The story of this small town welcoming, sheltering and caring for thousands of strangers who unexpectedly arrived on their doorstep was incredibly uplifting during such a horrific event. The play had a different level of intensity unlike anything I’ve experienced due to the atmosphere in the theatre. Sitting there with New Yorkers, many of whom may have lost family or friends in the terrorist attack 16 years ago, was severely emotional. The play however was inspirational and focused on the gratitude of stranded civilians and the tightknit bonds that were formed through the devastating experience. If you’re in New York, or even if you aren’t, this one is worth the trip!

October 2017. HUGO IS HOME.

We just brought home a puppy! He is a French Bulldog from an AMAZING breeder located in central Oregon (Maia’s Mini Supremes). His color is fawn with very unique white markings, I mean, just check out those white booties! Our breeder could not have been more accommodating. Maia welcomed us into her home and impressively answered every question we could think of concerning our new companion. Having grown up with big Labrador Retrievers, I never thought I’d own an animal under 50 pounds. Yet here I am! Buying my first pet and it weighs 6 pounds as a pup, unlikely to ever exceed 30 pounds. And I could not be more excited! We can already tell he is going to have the best personality and that level of cuteness will fill my everyday with smiles from ear to ear. Hugo Franc Berney, welcome to the family.

September 2017. Indonesia.

Travel. I don’t think there is any better way to spend time or money. I love traveling and have been exploring this planet all my life, or at least as much as my schedule will allow. My latest venture took place during the first two weeks of September. My partner and I flew into Jakarta, located on the island of Java in Indonesia. We quickly routed ourselves to Yogyakarta, which is known as one of the cultural epicenters of Indonesia. There we observed the biggest Buddhist temple in the world (Borobudur) and one of the largest Hindu temples (Prambanan)! We then spent a week in Bali, surfing in Kuta and hanging out in Ubud. Now that was a breath of fresh air. All the while, the Indonesian people were the nicest people I have ever met. It might have something to do with tourism underpinning the economy… BUT STILL. So nice.

Lastly, we took another hopper flight over to Singapore. Wow, what a clean, well-designed city. If you end up there for whatever reason, you MUST check out the Gardens by the Bay and of course the observation deck on the Marina Bay Sands. Coincidentally, our visit also fell on the weekend of the legendary Singapore Grand Prix: Singapore F1 – Formula 1 Night Race. So that was pretty neat. What a trip. I’d recommend it to anyone. Safe travels!
